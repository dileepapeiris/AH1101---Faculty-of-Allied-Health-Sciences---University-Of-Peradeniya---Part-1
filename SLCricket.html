Sri Lanka national cricket

Sri Lanka national Cricket Team is one of the full members of the International Cricket Council (ICC). Sri Lanka international Cricket has achieved significant success over the years. The team first played international cricket (as Ceylon) in 1926 and was later awarded Test status in 1982, which made Sri Lanka the eighth Test cricket playing nation. The team is administered by Sri Lanka Cricket.

The current captain of the Sri Lankan cricket team in Test matches is Dimuth Karunaratne. He is the captain of the team in limited over versions as well.
