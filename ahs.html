Faculty of Allied Health Sciences

Faculty of AHS was inaugurated on 16th July 2007. The faculty comprises of five departments conducting B.Sc. degree programmes in Physiotherapy, Nursing, Medical Laboratory Science, Radiography Radiotherapy and Pharmacy. As the faculty offers professional degree programmes, students are introduced to basic concepts in medical sciences followed by mandatory hospital based training.

Departments

  • Department of Physiotherapy
  • Department of Nursing
  • Department of Medical Laboratory Science
  • Department of Radiography
  • Department of Pharmacy
